**国外的很多大网站都会使用RECAPTCHA保证安全性，所以本文将教大家如何`优雅地显示RECAPTCHA认证`**

1. 文章插图 打开您的浏览器，以edge为例  
   ![优雅地显示RECAPTCHA认证](https://web.archive.org/web/20221001155658im_/https://ited.ml/content/uploadfile/202208/5ba71659929161.png)

2. 点击右上角的三个点，点击拓展或者[直接前去](https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons)安装拓展  
   ![优雅地显示RECAPTCHA认证](https://web.archive.org/web/20221001155658im_/https://ited.ml/content/uploadfile/202208/e7e51659929310.png)

3. 在拓展页面搜索"Header Editor"或[直接前去](https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons/detail/header-editor/afopnekiinpekooejpchnkgfffaeceko)，然后点击"安装拓展"，在弹出的提示框轻点确定  
   ![优雅地显示RECAPTCHA认证](https://web.archive.org/web/20221001155658im_/https://ited.ml/content/uploadfile/202208/1efe1659930058.png)  
   ![优雅地显示RECAPTCHA认证](https://web.archive.org/web/20221001155658im_/https://ited.ml/content/uploadfile/202208/53181659930155.png)

4. 打开[插件的设置页面](extension://djbcdihpmcbpkljpjibeiedjenilallo/options/options.html)，点击"导出和导入"，下载规则处输入`https://ited.ml/content/uploadfile/202208/685d1659930300.txt`，然后点击![优雅地显示RECAPTCHA认证](https://web.archive.org/web/20221001155658im_/https://ited.ml/content/uploadfile/202208/68801659930655.png)  
   ![优雅地显示RECAPTCHA认证](https://web.archive.org/web/20221001155658im_/https://ited.ml/content/uploadfile/202208/15151659930576.png)

5. 最后在下方点击导入即可  
   ![优雅地显示RECAPTCHA认证](https://web.archive.org/web/20221001155658im_/https://ited.ml/content/uploadfile/202208/thum-0a701659930755.png)  
   ![优雅地显示RECAPTCHA认证](https://web.archive.org/web/20221001155658im_/https://ited.ml/content/uploadfile/202208/thum-ceec1659930816.png)

教程至此
