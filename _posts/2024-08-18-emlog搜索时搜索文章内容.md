**emlog的内置搜索程序默认仅支持搜索标题,我们需要让其一并检索文章内容.**

1. 打开`\include\controller\search_controller.php`  
![emlog搜索时搜索文章内容](https://web.archive.org/web/20240829115722im_/https://ited.top/content/uploadfile/202408/thum-f6b81723941776.png)

2. 找到
```php
$sqlSegment = "and title like '%$keyword%'";
```
复制一份,在在第二个双引号前加入
```php
or content like '%{$keyword}%'
```
然后把原来的代码注释掉.  
![emlog搜索时搜索文章内容](https://web.archive.org/web/20240829115722im_/https://ited.top/content/uploadfile/202408/thum-8d8d1723941951.png)

3. 保存,大功告成,记得自己去测试.
